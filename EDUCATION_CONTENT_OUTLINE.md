## Content Development Guidelines

### Lesson Structure
Each lesson should include:
1. **Introduction** - What you'll learn
2. **Core Content** - Main teaching material
3. **Practical Examples** - Real-world scenarios
4. **Step-by-Step Guides** - Hands-on instructions
5. **Best Practices** - Do's and Don'ts
6. **Quick Reference** - Summary cards
7. **Practice Exercise** - Apply what you learned
8. **Next Steps** - Preview of next lesson

### Writing Style
- ‚úÖ Simple, clear language
- ‚úÖ Short paragraphs (3-5 lines)
- ‚úÖ Bullet points for lists
- ‚úÖ Bold for emphasis
- ‚úÖ Code blocks for technical content
- ‚úÖ Real-world examples
- ‚úÖ Security warnings where appropriate
- ‚ùå Jargon without explanation
- ‚ùå Overly technical details
- ‚ùå Assumptions about prior knowledge

### Content Standards
- **Length**: 15-30 minutes per lesson
- **Reading Level**: 8th grade (general audience)
- **Examples**: At least 2-3 per lesson
- **Exercises**: 1 practical exercise per lesson
- **Visuals**: Diagrams, screenshots (when possible)
- **Updates**: Review quarterly for accuracy
